      MySwitch Propriétés

      Propriétés

      sFirmware Gets or sets the firmware version of the switch.
      sGamme Gets or sets the product range or series of the switch.
      sInterfaceX1 Gets or sets the X1 interface address.
      sName Gets or sets the switch name.
      sReference Gets or sets the hardware reference of the switch.
      sVlanX1 Gets or sets the VLAN associated with X1.
