      MyVariator Propriétés

      Propriétés

      sGamme Gets or sets the product range or series of the variator.
      sInterfaceX1 Gets or sets the X1 interface address.
      sMasterName Gets or sets the name of the master device associated with this variator.
      sName Gets or sets the name of the variator.
      sReference Gets or sets the hardware reference of the variator.
      sVlanX1 Gets or sets the VLAN associated with X1.
